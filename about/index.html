About

Welcome to Per48.co!

It is a place for me to publish NBA related data analysis. Without blog scripts I create would just stay forever on my local directory, so I decided to set up a site and show my work to anyone interested. I hope you will enjoy/be interested enough to give feedback.

I hope I will be able to publish something interesting or revealing at least once a week. Fingers crossed!

My name is Patrick Chodowski.

I love NBA and I love R and data analysis. My favourite team is and always will be Denver Nuggets.

Last year I managed to finish economics/data analysis studies with master thesis about using neural networks to forecast winners of NBA games. At this moment I am working as a BI/Data science developer for one of the coolest IGaming companies on Malta.

For years contributed to Polish Denver Nuggets blog by writing game recaps. Currently writing only here.
